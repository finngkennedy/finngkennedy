Hi, my name is Finn and I just graduated from Cornell University with an Engineering Physics degree. I am mainly interested in experimental condensed matter physics. For the past couple years I worked with the Ralph group in the physics department, where I focused on building functioning Van der Waals heterostructures using sliding ferroelectrics, requiring experimental techniques such as E-beam lithography, atomic force microscopy, and Raman spectroscopy. Additionally, I held a position as a researcher in the Barstow lab for several years working on engineering novel metabolic pathways into fast growing bacteria. Outside of work/school, I love to watch movies, listen to music, and go running.
